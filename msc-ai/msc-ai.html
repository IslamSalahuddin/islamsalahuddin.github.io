The MSc AI Student

Hello! This is me Islam, the MSc AI student at the University of Essex Online. Here, I add my reflections and portfolio on the modules of my MSc program.

Overall Reflections

I started the MSc AI program at the University of Essex Online in January 2025, with an expected graduation date of January 2027. Considering the amount of investment it requires, I had three goals when I enrolled in this program:

  • To extend and boost my career in data analytics (my 'Data Guy' side) with knowledge, skills and academic recognition related to data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence, allowing for better potential career growth and opportunities.
  • To open up the doors for a potential opportunity where my creative side and 'Data Guy' side can finally meet and twine their paths in the wide realm of AI possibilities.
  • To initiate chances of pursuing further academic studies in the field of AI, possibly a PhD, especially regarding its creative applications and philosophical implications.
The program consists of six modules, each lasting three months, followed by a final thesis project that lasts for six months.
