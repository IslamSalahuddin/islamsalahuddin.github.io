## About

Hi, this is Islam Salahuddin. I currently work as a data consultant and data analytics trainer at a strategy consultancy company, after spending three years within the same company as a data analytics researcher (with the title of a data analyst). I'm also a Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) and I have several practitioner certifications. You can view my [LinkedIn profile here](https://www.linkedin.com/in/islamsalahuddin/).

But my life does not revolve around data only. I do creative writing from time to time, which ranges from fiction and non-fiction, and I work on projects that combine the two. Some of my writing work is reflected on my LinkedIn profile as well. I have some published books that you can see on my [Goodreads author profile](https://www.goodreads.com/islamsalahuddin).

My interests fall within the pack of arts and culture, including literature, cinema, music, paintings and philosophy. But I also have interest in technology, for both its practice and what it means. Generally speaking, I am a curious person and I like to connect things that I do not see the justification for its isolation in the first place.

I'm also studying for a master's degree in AI at the University of Essex Online.
